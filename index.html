HOME SWEET HOME

I was raised in Henderson, NV, a beautiful city right next to Las Vegas. This meant vacations were either to California to Disneyland and the beach or to the mountains of Utah to visit snow. Having been born in '92, I've had the pleasures of experiencing the early technologies of the 90s and then welcoming the age of technology in the 2000s.

BOLIVIA

I took 2 years out of my life to be an LDS missionary and serve the people of Bolivia. There I had to learn Spanish and even Quechua to get by. Adapting to the Bolivian culture - food, living conditions, and social environment was difficult, but well worth it. They have become my family and will always have a place in my heart.

COLLEGE YEARS

One day while studying at BYU, Mark Zuckerberg, founder of Facebook, came to speak at a forum. He mentioned that double majoring in Computer Science and Psychology helped with his success. That's what I wanted to do. I majored in Psychology (business Minor) and went to DevMoutain, a programming bootcamp.

PURSING MY PASSION

I had the great opportunity to mentor students at DevMountain in Web Development. I not only helped students learn to code, but gave lectures on various javascript or programming topics while developing new tools for DevMountain. I then took a job at Jolt, a startup in Orem, UT. There, I've mastered React, Redux, Immutability and React Native.

WHAT'S NEXT

Whether it is building desktop and mobile apps with my brothers or helping aspiring programmers with their apps, computer programming has become part of my life. My wife even gets into it as she gives her input on the design of the app. I am truly excited for whatever the future holds for my family and I.
